From the facts we have observed, the two main causes of climate change are

  1. The greenhouse effects
  2. Deforestation

    The greenhouse effects

    On Earth, human activities are changing the natural greenhouse. Over the last century the burning of fossil fuels like coal and oil has increased the concentration of atmospheric carbon dioxide (CO2). This happens because the coal or oil burning process combines carbon with oxygen in the air to make CO2. To a lesser extent, the clearing of land for agriculture, industry, and other human activities has increased concentrations of greenhouse gases.

    For this reason, we found out some solutions to limit the amount of atmospheric carbon dioxide(CO2) , especially the one released from burning fossil fuels, coal and oil.

    Southeast Asia is one of the most vulnerable regions of the world to climate change. In the absence of climate action, the region will be increasingly exposed to hotter temperatures, more destructive storms, greater flooding in some areas, and more droughts in others.

    We also need to consider the following perspective for reducing greenhouse effects in addition to increasing renewabel energy resources.
    1. Law enforcement
      Each nation must have policies, strategies and action plans for climate change.
    2. Public awareness
      If people do not know about the climate change and its effects , they will not prepare and coordinate in achieving the targeted goals for the mitigation of climate change. Public awareness should be promoted as much as possible.
    3. Technology
      Technologies for renewable energy have a great deal of benefits for climate change. Moreover, there are a lot of high technologies available today such as data analysis , remote sensing and other IT technologies. Using these technologies will make the progress faster in an easier way than the traditional solutions.
    4. International cooperation and aids
      Climate change is not about one region , one nation. It is the problem of the whole world and international cooperation is necessary in any ways.

      The described idea is general and each nation must has its own plan and policies according to the situation of the country. We will describe about each country in the later articles.
